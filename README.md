# CHAPNETAI WEATHER

## National Weather Intelligence

Powered by NOAA • National Weather Service

CHAPNETAI Weather is a next-generation weather intelligence platform built to go beyond basic forecasts.

The mission is to combine official NOAA and National Weather Service data with ChapNetAI Watchman Intelligence to create a weather operations platform that helps users understand threats, prepare earlier, and make better decisions.

## Platform Identity

Name: CHAPNETAI Weather

Intelligence Layer: ChapNetAI Watchman Intelligence

## Core Vision

Most weather apps show weather data.

CHAPNETAI Weather is being built to explain weather risk.

The platform will provide forecasts, alerts, radar, national threat intelligence, plain-language briefings, decision-support tools, and a Weather Operations Center for local, regional, and national awareness.

## Planned Core Features

- Current conditions
- Hourly forecast
- Seven-day forecast
- NOAA / National Weather Service integration
- Active watches, warnings, and advisories

## Watchman Intelligence

Watchman Intelligence is the ChapNetAI analysis layer that sits on top of official weather data.

Planned Watchman features:

- National Threat Level
- Local Threat Level
- Outdoor Index
- Travel Index
- Construction Index
- Gardening Index
- Fishing Index
- Photography Conditions
- Severe Weather Timeline
- AI Situation Reports
- Weather Operations Center
- Plain-language risk summaries
- Decision-support briefings

Watchman Intelligence does not replace official NOAA or National Weather Service forecasts, watches, warnings, or advisories.

Official weather alerts and forecasts remain the authoritative source.

## Disclaimer

CHAPNETAI Weather uses official weather data sources where available, including NOAA and the National Weather Service.

CHAPNETAI Weather and Watchman Intelligence are independent ChapNetAI systems and are not official National Weather Service products.

Always follow official guidance from NOAA, the National Weather Service, emergency management agencies, and local authorities during hazardous weather.
